Instructions for the game

  • Bugs are the enemies that Player should avoid.
  • Player can move in either direction i.e left, right, up and down.
  • Player has to collect all the collectable items i.e lives, gems star and keys to win the game.
  • After collecting all the items player should reach the top left block so as to win the game.
  • On collision with bugs will reduce the lives of the Player and Game will be finish if Lives becomes 'zero'.
